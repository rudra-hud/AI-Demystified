What is Artificial Intelligence?

A simple guide from AI Demystified

The Short Answer

AI (Artificial Intelligence) is computer systems that can perform tasks typically requiring human intelligence, like:

  • Recognizing patterns in data
  • Making decisions based on information
  • Understanding spoken or written language
  • Learning from experience

The Chef Analogy

Think of AI like a chef learning to cook:

  1. Recipe books = Data (what the AI learns from)
  2. Tasting dishes = Training (finding patterns)
  3. Cooking new meals = Prediction (doing its job)

Two Main Types of AI

1. Narrow AI

(What exists today)

Specialized in one task. Examples:

  • Face recognition
  • Spam filters
  • Voice assistants

2. General AI

(Still theoretical)

Human-like intelligence that could:

  • Reason about anything
  • Transfer knowledge
  • Think creatively

Key Point to Remember

AI isn't "alive" or conscious - it's sophisticated pattern recognition powered by:

  • Data (Information to learn from)
  • Algorithms (Mathematical instructions)
  • Computing Power (Processing capability)
